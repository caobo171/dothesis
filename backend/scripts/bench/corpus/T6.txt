Microservices utilize distributed architectures which inherently facilitate increased operational overhead during standard software deployment processes. Furthermore, these modular systems leverage network communication protocols which encompass significant latency and reliability challenges. Moreover, decentralized data management strategies necessitate complex synchronization mechanisms to ensure consistent information integrity across all services. Additionally, monitoring multiple independent components requires sophisticated observability tools to maintain optimal system performance and security. Consequently, managing numerous distinct services demands extensive automation and specialized technical expertise for effective architectural governance.
